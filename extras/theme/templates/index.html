Welcome to this collection of articles, how-tos and announcements.

These pages are driven by the idea that the modern scientific method is now almost entirely dependent upon software, and that, in order to facilitate transparency and reproducibility, scientific software must be open source.

As a scientific technical lead at a leading natural sciences research institute, I'm fortunate to contribute to a body of tools that empower researchers and data scientists accross a broad spectrum of fields.

As well as an intimate knowledge of the rapidly changing technology landscape, development of useful scientific tools depends upon a soild understanding of the scientific problem space. For these reasons you will find these pages span a variety of topics, from being entirely technology focussed right through to being purely scientific in nature.

Open source tools that I have made significant contributions towards include:

conda-forge
repohealth.info
Iris
Shapely
conda-build-all
